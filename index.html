Our vision is to develop well rounded, confident and responsible individuals who aspire to achieve their full potential. We will do this by providing a welcoming, happy, safe and supportive learning environment in which everyone is equal and all achievements are celebrated.
Our foremost mission is to empower all students to embrace learning and to give their personal best and build their emotional, social and physical well-being.

Why us?

It's with us, where good becomes great!

Our mode of education is a complete circle which flows in a definite manner to maintain a stable motion of the students. Strong base is necessary for every start. We counsel each and every student right before their journey in our school. Interaction among students and teachers are built for good communication.

Once we get to know students, we follow a genuine plan to teach students. Making them to explore the informative world is one of our motives. The more we let us reach students, the more they go through it.
Ability of the student is tested to know what strategy of training must be followed for that specific student to share equal path with the rest. We offer special care for students who require more efforts to keep it up. You don't have to do it right, you just have to do something.

We give you our time to review in your performance to stick the pieces at the right place. Determined satisfaction is our key to make students feel better about themselves. Analysation takes the major part in the whole circle. “What's next?” is answered by this step. It makes us to know what is required and how we should act accordingly. This whole pattern of education is at high success rate.

Note from Principal

We, at Global Intelli School, ensure high quality education which motivates and empowers our, students to be lifelong learners and the productive members of the society. Success for all students is ensured at our school by the delivery of an enriching, child centred, balanced and structured curriculum and a whole school focus on student welfare that focuses on the positive recognition of student achievements. Teachers are highly qualified and experienced educators who are committed to supporting each student's learning experience through quality instruction and guidance that addresses the needs of the individual child. Teachers work to provide students with differentiated support that address each student's specific needs both academically and socially. I whole-heartedly concern my student's pare of in and top of every fields. I would like the parents to know that my door is always open to discuss any concerns, questions, or ideas you may have regarding your child's education. We look forward to welcome you to be part of our learning community.
