Just Know Us

About Us

We are a budding Government authorized school with a motive to provide stress-free education. We, students and teachers with best guidance of the chair persons established three branches - Branch 1 with Integrated curriculum of State Board, CBSE and ICSE, Branch 2 & 3 with State Board curriculum. We are open from Nursery to Grade 10 with 36 as class strength. We step ourselves forward to provide free education to the students referred by non-governmental or other organisations and foundations.

Student-Teacher Bond

Students are engaged with Dedicated faculties to ensure the growth in safe environment. Personal and Intensive care for slow-learners is one of our priorities. 1:20 Teacher Student ratio makes the students to communicate better. Teachers help them to cultivate involvement, teamwork and independency.

Way to socialize has been our basic method to approach students in their first stage. We teach them that they must work hard, but that learning can be fun. We bond up in a way to sit back and enjoy in home with no worries about academic side of children.

Academic Facilities

We offer 1+1+1 mode of teaching i.e., Teaching + Work Session + Doubt Clarification. Most Experienced Lecturers has been appointed in Velocity Batch (Global Engineering Program for IIT Aspirants) and Gravity Batch (Global Medical Program for Medical Aspirants).

We follow Basic - Concept - Application method teaching with Cumulative and Grand Objective Test in both offline and online mode. We own personal software “Global E-Learn” to conduct online exams. We organize interactive sessions with achievers in various fields of our society. We have special spoken English classes to enhance the speech ability and early language training in English and Hindi to aid them in their prosperity.

Extra-Curriculum Facilities

Once in a week, students have assembly and sessions of games, drill, dance (western and classical), martial and yoga which refreshes their mind and make them to concentrate more in their studies. We encourage students in all fields of sports excluding Hockey and Rugby and to take part in inter and intra school competitions and to win back home. We are all open to respect children's activities which play a major role in their enthusiasm which stays a hidden secret for their success.

Infrastructure & Surroundings

School Campus is well built with well-furnished. Our integrated branch 1 is with Air-Conditioned class rooms. Greenery environment for Kinder-Garten for joy-filled learning. Libraries, Science and Computer Labs are available to cherish students in the way of practical learning. We have RO system and customized safety measures in case of emergency. Campus is under 24 x 7 CCTV Surveillance. Bus Facility Available.

Rules & Regulations

Students and Parents are requested to follow the regular timings and disciple inside and outside the school campus. Regular timings for all grades are from 8:30 AM to 3:30 PM. Special class will be held from 3:30 PM to 5:30 PM.

The purpose of dress code is to enhance the learning environment. For Girls, half hand piped yellow T-shirt with Checked Block & White Closed Collar with school logo stitched to the left of T-shirt. Black & White Checked Skirt with stitched bow with White socks and White Shoes. For Boys, half Hand piped yellow T-shirt with Checked Block & White Closed Collar with school logo stitched to the left of T-shirt. Black & White checked trousers with White socks and Black Shoes. (Materials will be provided. Picture reference for stitching is appreciated)
